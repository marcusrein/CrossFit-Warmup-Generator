Welcome to the Warmup Generator!

Please categorize your exercises in today's workout by "Easy" and "Tough".

"Easy" = An exercise you can perform for 10 reps or more.

"Tough" = An exercise you could perform for fewer than 10 reps.

FAQ: "So where do 'deadlifts' go?"

If you can do 20 deadlifts at the weight programmed in your workout (due to the deadlifts being lighter), you should put deadlifts in the "Easy" category.

If you can do just 5 deadlifts at the weight programmed by your workout (due to the deadlifts being heavier), then you should put deadlifts in the "Tough" category
